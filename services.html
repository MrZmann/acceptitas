Our Services


Acceptitas offers a wide range of virtual services to help students craft their most compelling narrative through all parts of the college application, with a special focus on the essays. Unlike other companies, all our services are incredibly personal (we’re students after all!), meaning we will work together 1-on-1 and always give you our full attention. Find descriptions of our services, prices, and packages below. Hover over a service image to view its price. Ready to get started? Visit our contact page to schedule a free consultation!

Strategy Consultations

Together we will examine your life experiences, interests, and aspirations to begin crafting your unique narrative through your essays and other application materials. After our meeting, you will receive an assessment of your current narrative, its strengths, areas for growth, and an action plan for refining your story across your application. Typically two-to-three hours total.

Narrative Assessment

We review your current application packet materials, provide constructive feedback in a 1-on-1 meeting, and help you identify and amplify your strongest narratives. Part of this process is our signature mock admissions read which we use as a diagnostic test of your narrative’s strength. Typically two hours total.

$49/hour


Mock Interviews

Learn how to lean into your strengths and naturally support your overall application narrative through our mock interviews, conducted by someone who has successful first-hand experience. Each mock interview comes with an after-action report that identifies actionable areas of growth for the big day. Typically two hours total.

$49 per application


Last Minute Editing

We provide last-minute essay and overall narrative feedback for driven students wanting to present the most compelling version of themself, and click the submit button with confidence.
